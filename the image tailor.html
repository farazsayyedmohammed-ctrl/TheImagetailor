Frequently Asked Questions

What is your turnaround time?

Our standard turnaround time for most photo edits is **24-48 hours**. For more complex projects like posters or videos, it may take a bit longer. We will provide an estimated delivery time after reviewing your request.

How do I send you my images or videos?

You can upload your files directly using the **Quick Booking** form on our homepage. If the files are too large, you can share them via a cloud service like Google Drive or Dropbox in the notes section.

Do you offer refunds if I am not satisfied?

Yes, customer satisfaction is our top priority. If the final edit does not meet your expectations, we offer free rework based on your feedback. In cases where we cannot meet your needs, we will discuss a refund.

What types of services do you offer?

We provide a wide range of services including photo retouching, background removal, custom designs for posters and social media posts, and professional reel & video editing. You can view a detailed list on our **Pricing Menu** page.
